FeedHenry

Node.js development platform for creating applications for iOS, Android, Windows Phone and Apache Cordova.

Features:

  • Allows BYOT (bring your own tools) development through FHC
  • Existing native, HTML5 or hybrid apps can be migrated to the platform
  • Projects can be hosted with public cloud services like Github, or locally
  • Includes a fully integrated Drag-and-Drop form builder for those who prefer to build apps that way
  • Choice of open toolkits

Pricing:

OpenShift customers can use it for at low initial cost, but for others there are no published prices
They charge by utilisation of the cloud so it can be as little as $1,000 a month to as much as $30,000 or more
Related article to pricing

Hosted Build Farm Service

  • The hosted Build Farm service provides builds for native and hybrid apps
  • Supports iOS, Android, Windows Phone, generating the build and digitally signing the apps
  • Eliminates the need to maintain device-specific hardware/software environments
